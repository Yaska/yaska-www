Essential

We maintain your computer on a constant basis.

We will take over all the maintenance tasks for your computer in remote. We’ll make the updates, check the status of your RAM Memory and storage situation and many more.

By constantly monitoring your network we will be able to eliminate the small problems in your computer that give you headaches and that could potentially lead to bigger problems.

We will send you a monthly report with actions made and recommendations to optimize the performance of your computers.

Security

Your computer makes its backups entirely automatically

Crash Plan PROe is a program that will automatically back up all your valuable data, such as work documents or pictures. If you loose a file or even accidentally erase it, you will be able to retrieve it by yourself, or ask us to do so in remote.

Kerio Connect is an amazing piece of software that makes it possible to always have all your information with you, no matter where you are. This program makes sure that all your e-mails, agendas and contacts keep synchronised with your different devices, such as your computers, iPhones, iPads and Blackberries.

You can also share your agenda with your colleagues, making it possible for them to know when you are available and even add appointments for you in your agenda.

Development

You name it, we make it!
We provide all kinds of development services, from websites to iOS applications and many more.

Awesome and User-friendly design

There are two things on which we don’t compromise:
We are deeply convinced that a design has to look nice and create positive emotions. This is why we spend a lot of time checking out the latest trends in colors and typography. We also pay a lot of attention on adapting our design to our customer’s preferences and style.

The second thing is creating a user-friendly page. We create interfaces that are accessible and easy to use for everyone.

Flexible and Clean Markup

We use the latest technologies that allows us to be flexible to your changing demands. So instead of delivering you a fixed result, you can still contact us to change or add certain features even months after the delivery. Our developers pride themselves on writing in «clean Markup». A great benefit of this way of working is that this makes your website highly visible in search engines, like Google, making it easier for potential costumers to find their way to your services.

Integration with your system

We can provide an information flow between your web or iOS (or other) applications and your central system. This is done with flexible parameters, managing who can retrieve what information on what moment. It also goes the other way: all the information that is collected through your web application, gets integrated and structured right away into your internal system.
