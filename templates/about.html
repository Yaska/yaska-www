Hello, we are Yaska

We started out with just one guy, Julien, crossing the whole country to offer technical support. Through the years we had to expand, and now we're 6 people and several freelancers, offering technical support and development services. Today we're a strong team, passionate about our work.

Wether you contact us for technical support or development, we try to come up with comprehensive and high-quality solutions. We like a challenge and are able to think outside the box to find solutions tailored to your needs.

This strategy has encouraged us to become part of the Apple Consultants Network, which is a network for independently owned companies that offer technical support for Apple products.

Apple applies strict requirements for inclusion, making sure we can guarantee you a high level of expertise.
